## üí° Recommendations

Based on the dashboard analysis, the following actions are recommended:

1. **Prioritize Online Promotions** - Focus marketing efforts on online channels in high-performing cities like Noida and Delhi
2. **Optimize Purchase Strategy** - Review and adjust purchasing for under-margin categories to reduce losses
3. **Inventory Management** - Increase stock levels for high-demand items (lamps, wheat flour, table runners)
4. **Routine Monitoring** - Use the dashboard regularly to track monthly sales momentum and loss reduction progress
5. **Channel Strategy** - While maintaining offline presence, continue investing in online channel optimization
